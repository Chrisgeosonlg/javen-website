Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the main difference between Full Management and Javen OS?+

Full-Service Management is a comprehensive physical and digital solution. Javen deploys the software, collects rent, manages tenant relations, runs estate logistics, and dispatches maintenance teams.

Javen OS is a software license only. You remain physically responsible for your units — Javen provides the premium digital platform for rent tracking, invoicing, and contract storage.

Why don't you publish flat percentages on your website?+

A flat percentage often penalizes landlords unfairly. A 50-unit commercial complex operates on entirely different economics than a 4-unit luxury apartment block. By customizing your fixed fee based on unit volume, asset type, and service tier, we ensure you only pay for the exact level of oversight your property requires.

How does Javen manage the estate service charges for managed buildings?+

Javen collects service charges directly from tenants and handles the timely payout and scheduling for building water, common area LUKU, trash collection, landscaping, fumigation, and security teams. Landlords never have to chase tenants for utility contributions or deal with sudden service cut-offs.

How does Javen handle deep cleaning when a tenant moves out?+

For Full-Service clients, when a space is vacated Javen automatically conducts a thorough digital visual inspection. Through your Owner Portal you can review the turnaround report and instantly authorize our professional deep-cleaning crew. Javen OS clients can access our vetted vendor list but must coordinate cleaning independently.

Are there any hidden setup fees or onboarding costs?+

Absolutely not. Onboarding your portfolio, migrating existing tenant data, and setting up your Owner Login dashboard is completely free for both plans. Your transparent fixed fee only activates once your account is fully built out and your active management or software access begins.
