About JAVEN

Born from Experience.
Built for the Modern Property Owner.

A PropTech company founded in Dar es Salaam in 2026 — built to replace fragmented property management processes with a centralized, technology-driven platform.

Our Story

In 2016, while working within a portfolio of over 220 units, our founder experienced firsthand the operational challenges of managing properties through spreadsheets. Despite the scale of the operation, every rent payment, lease agreement, tenant record, and vacancy was tracked manually in Microsoft Excel.

Knowing who had paid rent, which units were vacant, or when a lease was due for renewal often required hours of administrative work. The process was time-consuming, prone to human error, and lacked the visibility that modern property owners and managers needed.

The turning point came in 2020 during a master's program in Sweden. Immersed in a fully digitized real estate ecosystem, the contrast was immediate. Rent, invoices, maintenance, and tenant communications managed seamlessly through one platform. No paperwork. No endless follow-ups. No friction.

"The experience highlighted what property management could become when supported by the right technology."

Founder, JAVEN — Sweden, 2020

Upon returning to Tanzania in 2023, many of the same operational challenges remained. Even large portfolios continued relying on spreadsheets, manual reporting, and disconnected systems — limiting efficiency, transparency, and decision-making at every level.

After years of observing these challenges, studying global PropTech best practices, and exploring the growing field of property technology, Javen was founded in 2026.

We built Javen OS to replace fragmented processes with a centralized, technology-driven platform — giving owners, managers, and tenants a smarter way to interact with real estate. Today, Javen combines professional management services with modern technology to streamline operations, automate routine tasks, and provide real-time visibility into property performance.

Our goal is simple: help owners maximize returns, protect investments, and make informed decisions with confidence — while delivering a seamless experience for tenants from move-in to move-out.
